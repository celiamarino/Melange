Welcome to Melange! We are glad you are here!

Melange is a subscription style bakery that caters to YOUR needs! We have our varied product selection, which you can adjust to your own needs and preferences. This includes having vegan, gluten free, kosher, and dairy free options! In the moment, we ship to addresses within 20 miles from the University of Southern California, but stay tuned to see our expansion to other campuses around the United States very soon! If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at info@melange.com.
