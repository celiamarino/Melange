USC Team

Who doesn't want a beautiful and delicious box of fresh baked goods on their doorstep every week? When a team of USC students discovered they had no subscription bakery to turn to for their sweet cravings the idea of Melange was born. Let us take you to Paris with our Parisian inspired bakery boxes.  We remain authentic in our mission to create fresh bakeries made with simple and locally sourced ingredients. We pick and peel our own apples, roll out our own pastry, pick our own chocolate...  We operate out of a small kitchen in Downtown LA to ensure each batch is made to perfection.  Our mission is simple and we thank you for being part of this beautiful journey.
