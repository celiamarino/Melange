Frequently Asked Questions

What is Melange?

Melange is a subscription bakery that offers handcrafted, locally sourced baked goods delivered to your door on a weekly basis.

How does Melange work?

Simply choose your subscription frequency and select your delivery location. We will deliver a selection of our fresh baked goods to your door each week.

What types of products does Melange offer?

We offer a variety of baked goods including bread, pastries, cookies, and more. Our selection may vary from week to week depending on availability and seasonality.

Where does Melange source its ingredients?

We source our ingredients from local producers and suppliers whenever possible. We believe in supporting our local community and using the freshest, highest quality ingredients.

How often will I receive my Melange delivery?

Delivery frequency is based on your selected subscription plan. We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly delivery options.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

Please contact us and let us know about your dietary restrictions. We will do our best to accommodate your needs.

How does Melange ensure the freshness of its products?

We take great care in packaging and shipping our products to ensure they arrive in the best possible condition. Our products are shipped in insulated boxes and may include ice packs to maintain their freshness during transit.
